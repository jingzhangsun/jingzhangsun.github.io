## Research Directions

### Low-count Cardiac SPECT/PET Quantification

We develop AI-enabled quantitative imaging methods for myocardial perfusion SPECT/PET under reduced dose, shortened acquisition, and gated imaging conditions.

### AI-enabled Attenuation Correction and Image Restoration

We study physics-informed and clinically constrained models for attenuation correction, denoising, deblurring, and quantitative consistency in nuclear medicine imaging.

### ROI-free Dynamic Nuclear Medicine Imaging

We develop learning-based methods that estimate functional parameters directly from dynamic nuclear medicine image sequences, reducing dependence on manual ROI delineation.
